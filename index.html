Our Story

Shorty Got Low Low Low Dumplings!

Get Low Dumplings is a Bay Area based dumpling company, with an aspiration to create creative, delicious dumplings. Ever since I was a girl, I've loved food. Sure, I loved eating food. But there was something magical about its connective abilities. I mean, it's no wonder that terms like "breaking bread" or "a seat at the table" alluded to how sharing meals has a unique ability to connect folks. For me, it's a way I've seen and experienced my family's love when words or other gestures were too hard. After graduating with a degree in Human Biology and some failed attempts at white collar 'adulating', I still felt compelled by the food industry.


Get Low Dumplings was born of a personal desire to combine food, human connection, and creativity. We try to integrate the flavors and experiences of Oakland's melting pot into our recipes. Though we're just beginning to build out our dumpling empire, our end game is to create the Bay Area's premier dumpling brand, with a focus on seasonality, creativity, and customer experience!
